FAQ

How much does it cost ?
Subtitler will always be completely free to use. The project is completely open source and we welcome contributions if you'd like to help out!
How does it work ?
Just select video file and select/click on one of item in the list, the subtitle will be downloaded and renamed at the video's location. Play the video and subtitle should work automatically.
Where are these subtitles from ?
All the subtitles and IMDb title info are fetched from Open subtitles API.
Getting wrong subtitles & info ?
Since all info are fetched from Open subtitles, Some Info might not be accurate. Please consider contributing info to Open subtitles.
Does Subtitler track anything from my PC ?
No, we don't track anything from your PC. Be sure to go through Open subtitles Disclaimer
